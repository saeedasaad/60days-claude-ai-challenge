## What I Did

For Day 13, I explored Claude Connectors by connecting Claude AI with Indeed to automate job discovery and career analysis.

The exercise demonstrated how AI can use external data sources to identify relevant opportunities, analyze market demand, and create a more strategic job search process.

### Step 1: Connect Indeed to Claude

* Opened Claude Connectors
* Connected my Indeed account
* Verified access to job market data

### Step 2: Create Professional Profile

* Generated a professional profile based on my background
* Defined my skills, experience, and career goals
* Prepared a profile for job matching

### Step 3: Define Job Search Criteria

* Selected target roles
* Defined preferred technologies and skills
* Specified job preferences and requirements

### Step 4: AI Job Discovery

* Allowed Claude to search for matching opportunities
* Reviewed discovered jobs
* Evaluated match scores and recommendations

### Step 5: Career Analysis

* Reviewed skill-gap analysis
* Studied hiring trends and market demand
* Identified growth opportunities and learning priorities
