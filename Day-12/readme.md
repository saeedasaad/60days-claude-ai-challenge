##  What I Did
 
For **Day 12**, I used Claude AI to generate a complete **Job Search & Personal Branding Toolkit** based on my resume and target role.
 
The process focused on improving career positioning, recruiter outreach, ATS compatibility, and identifying growth opportunities.
 
---
 
## Step-by-Step Process
 
### Step 1: Resume Analysis
 
- Uploaded my latest resume
- Selected my target role
- Added a relevant Job Description (JD)
- Generated ATS analysis and optimization recommendations
### Step 2: Skill Gap Analysis
 
- Compared my resume against the target role requirements
- Identified missing technical and professional skills
- Received recommendations for improving job readiness
### Step 3: Recruiter Outreach
 
- Generated personalized recruiter outreach messages
- Created networking messages for LinkedIn connections
- Improved professional communication strategy
### Step 4: Personal Branding
 
- Created a professional positioning statement
- Developed a stronger personal brand narrative
- Improved profile presentation and career visibility
### Step 5: Career Strategy
 
- Identified strengths and competitive advantages
- Highlighted growth opportunities
- Created a roadmap for future career development
