    Workflow summary

    Discovery locks scope and gets a contract signed. Planning and design produce an approved architecture and Figma file before a line of feature code exists. Setup and core development are where AI pair-programming tools save the most hours — expect these two stages to eat roughly half the project timeline. Backend integration, QA, and client review close the loop before the app ever reaches a store listing. Deployment is mostly a waiting game for App Store review, and handoff is where a freelancer either earns a referral or loses one — documentation and a maintenance offer are what separate the two.
