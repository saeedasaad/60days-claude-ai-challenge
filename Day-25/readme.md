## Project Overview

For Day 25 of the **#60DaysOfClaude** challenge, I explored how Claude can generate complete business simulation applications from a single prompt.

Using the provided **AI Shark Tank Simulator** prompt, Claude generated a fully interactive web application that allows entrepreneurs to pitch startup ideas, answer investor questions, and receive realistic investment decisions.

The simulator mimics the experience of presenting a startup in front of investors and helps founders better understand how investors evaluate business opportunities.

---

## Features

The generated application includes:

* Startup idea submission form
* Multiple AI investor personas
* Interactive investor Q&A sessions
* Automated startup evaluation
* Detailed startup scorecards
* Investment recommendations
* Valuation estimation
* Funding suggestions
* Leaderboard functionality
* Downloadable pitch reports
* Responsive user interface

---

## Startup Evaluation Criteria

The simulator evaluates startup ideas based on:

* Problem-Solution Fit
* Market Opportunity
* Revenue Potential
* Competitive Advantage
* Founder Execution Ability
* Scalability Potential
* Customer Validation
* Investment Readiness

---

## Investor Simulation Experience

During the simulation, AI investors asked questions related to:

* Market size
* Customer pain points
* Revenue model
* Competitive landscape
* Go-To-Market strategy
* Growth assumptions
* Product differentiation
* Funding requirements

This provided a realistic startup pitching experience similar to actual investor meetings.

## Generated Insights

After completing the simulation, the application generated:

* Overall Startup Score
* Category-wise Scores
* Strength Analysis
* Weakness Analysis
* Risk Assessment
* Improvement Suggestions
* Suggested Valuation
* Investment Recommendation

These insights helped evaluate the startup's overall viability.
