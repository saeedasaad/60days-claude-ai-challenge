For Day 7, I selected the **Experienced Professional** resume optimization workflow.

Claude refined my resume by focusing on:

The final output was a more structured and impactful resume aligned with modern hiring standards.

##  Key Improvements

### Before

* Generic responsibility-based bullets
* Limited measurable impact
* Basic keyword usage
* Less structured formatting

### After

* Action-oriented achievement statements
* Quantified results (numbers/impact added)
* Strong ATS keyword alignment
* Cleaner and professional structure
* Improved recruiter readability

---

## Key Learnings

* ATS systems filter resumes before recruiters see them
* Strong action verbs improve impact and clarity
* Measurable achievements increase credibility
* Keyword alignment improves job match visibility
* Clean, simple formatting works best for ATS
* AI can significantly improve resume quality and structure
