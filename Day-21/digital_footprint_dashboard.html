Final Verdict

⚠️ Moderate to Significant Privacy Risk
You maintain a digital footprint across 15 applications operated by 11 parent companies. Your highest-risk exposures are behavioral profiling on social platforms (Instagram, Snapchat, TikTok) and search/analytics tracking via Google. While your ecosystem is typical for an active internet user, the concentration of data collection with Meta and Google—combined with limited privacy controls you've likely enabled—suggests moderate vulnerability to targeted advertising, data breaches, and identity risks.

Key recommendations: (1) Audit third-party access on Google and Meta accounts immediately. (2) Enable 2FA across all services, starting with payment and email accounts. (3) Reduce data sharing with social platforms through stricter privacy settings. (4) Monitor your privacy checkups (Google Privacy Checkup, Meta Privacy Center) quarterly. (5) Consider selectively migrating to privacy-respecting alternatives for communication and search.
